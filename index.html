Core Capabilities

Comprehensive digital media authentication for mission-critical operations.

Cryptographic Validation

SHA-256 and HMAC-based authentication of digital media with blockchain-anchored provenance tracking. Immutable audit trails ensure evidential integrity for legal and operational use.

Manipulation Detection

Advanced algorithms identify digital alterations, deepfakes, and synthetic media. Multi-spectrum analysis detects compression artifacts, inconsistencies, and adversarial perturbations.

Real-Time Processing

Sub-50ms verification latency for operational environments. Distributed architecture scales to process thousands of media files simultaneously with consistent performance.

Chain of Custody

Complete metadata preservation and tamper-evident logging. Every access, modification, and transfer is cryptographically signed and recorded for forensic accountability.

Technology Foundation

Military-grade authentication built on proven cryptographic standards and distributed systems architecture.

Cryptographic Integrity

SHA-256 hashing with HMAC authentication. AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit. Digital signatures using ECDSA with P-256 curves for non-repudiation.

Distributed Architecture

Horizontally scalable verification nodes with automatic failover. Geographic distribution ensures <99.99% uptime with sub-regional latency optimization.

Metadata Preservation

EXIF, XMP, and IPTC metadata extraction and validation. Geospatial, temporal, and device fingerprinting for complete provenance documentation.

API Integration

RESTful and gRPC APIs for seamless integration with existing workflows. SDKs available for Python, JavaScript, Java, and C++ development environments.

Operational Applications

Deployed across defense, intelligence, law enforcement, and critical infrastructure sectors.

Defense & Intelligence

Validate reconnaissance imagery, SIGINT data, and operational intelligence. Detect adversarial manipulation and ensure evidentiary standards for classification-appropriate workflows.

Law Enforcement

Authenticate digital evidence for criminal investigations and court proceedings. Maintain chain of custody from collection through prosecution with cryptographic audit trails.

Critical Infrastructure

Secure SCADA systems, industrial control networks, and operational technology environments. Verify firmware updates and command authentication for power, water, and transportation systems.

Media & Journalism

Combat misinformation with verifiable source attribution. Authenticate user-generated content and prevent publication of manipulated imagery or deepfake media.
